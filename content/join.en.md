## Join Us

The lab welcomes students from industrial design, product design, transportation design, robotics engineering, mechanical engineering, artificial intelligence, computer science, interaction design, digital media, and related interdisciplinary backgrounds.

We encourage problem-driven interdisciplinary research, with emphasis on methodological innovation, prototyping, and application validation. Students are supported to explore topics in complex system design, bio-inspired robotics, supportive embodied systems, future mobility, and intelligent interaction.

### Students Who May Fit

- Interested in intelligent design, robot design, interaction design, or AI-assisted design
- Willing to connect theoretical questions, design methods, and prototype implementation
- With backgrounds in design, engineering, computer science, psychology, human factors, or digital media
- Interested in interdisciplinary research and design practice grounded in real-world problems

### Possible Topics

- Knowledge-driven conceptual design and design reasoning
- Human-AI collaborative design and generative AI-assisted design
- Multimodal interaction, eye tracking, and human state sensing
- Supportive embodied systems, interactive devices, and robotic product design
- Future mobility, intelligent cockpit, and transportation interaction design
